The Mozilla Media Player (mozmp) is an attempt at creating an audio and video player much like Real Networks Real Player using mozilla components. The hope is that one day mozmp will support a bevy of audio formats, and a gaggle of video formats. And a boat load of streaming options. All without any nasty copyright, patent, or lawsuit problems. Until that day, mozmp will have to settle for just playing videos using either libmpeg3 or smpeg.

WARNING!
mozmp is in its infancy. There is barely the beginnings of the UI in CVS. And the .xpi most likely won't even install. Please do not file a bug report about this. But please do submit a patch that fixes this to the mailing list. :) Thanks!
