The Mozilla Media Player (mozmp) is an attempt at creating an audio and video player much like Real Networks Real Player using mozilla components. The hope is that one day mozmp will support a bevy of audio formats, and a gaggle of video formats. And a boat load of streaming formats. All without any nasty copyright, patent, or lawsuit problems.

Until that day, mozmp will have to settle for just playing videos using either libmpeg3 or smpeg.
